Hello! Welcome to Tele-photo, a game of image association based on image tagging technology. The goal of the game is to find and click on an image with a tag matching the destination on the upper right.

1. Click "Start a Game"

2. A beginning image wil appear in the upper left, as well as a series of images found on Pixabay matching a randomly generated starting tag.

3. Click on a square image to genereate more tags, and in turn generate more images in this field based on its tags.

4. By using association, your goal is to work towards finding and clicking on an image that matches the destination tag to the upper left.

5. Due to the unique nature of ever pictures tags, some pictures will generate next to no images. (We are actively eliminating tags that do not produce any images).

6. That said, some images will generate more tags than others.

7. You have 120 seconds and 15 clicks. Your score is based on how few clicks and how much time it takes to win.

8. There is an inherent and inextricable element of luck in this game, and though some images may appear to show a similar image, the destination tag, you may still have to find an image that more clearly represents the tag shown.

9. Luck aside, skill is imperative, all destination tags come directly from the technology that produces the images in this app.

10. If you enjoy, feel free to share, but please do not distribute publicly without first contacting me, as there are limits on my API!

This app has not yet been scaled for the average browser size of 1024×768, but neatly formats upon zooming out to workable a size, anywhere from 50-75%, <⌘ shift +> Enjoy!
